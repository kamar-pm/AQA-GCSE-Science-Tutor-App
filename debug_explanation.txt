Let's dive into the fascinating world of cells.\n\n### Cells Overview\n\n- **Cells** are the basic building blocks of all living organisms.\n- They can be **prokaryotic**, like bacteria, or **eukaryotic**, like plant and animal cells.\n\n### Key Concepts\n\n- **Cell Membrane**: A protective layer that controls what enters and exits the cell.\n- **Nucleus**: The control center containing genetic material.\n- **Cytoplasm**: A jelly-like substance where cell activities occur.\n- **Mitochondria**: The powerhouse of the cell, providing energy.\n- **Ribosomes**: Sites of protein synthesis.\n\n### Differences Between Plant and Animal Cells\n\n- **Plant Cells**: Have a cell wall, chloroplasts, and a large central vacuole.\n- **Animal Cells**: Lack a cell wall and chloroplasts but have more lysosomes.\n\n### Cell Functions\n\n- **Growth**: Cells divide to help organisms grow.\n- **Reproduction**: Cells reproduce to form new cells.\n- **Response to Stimuli**: Cells can react to changes in their environment.\n\n"
